kendama-open

Kendama-open is a lightweight, mobile-first web application to diversify experience with kendama. It uses Flask repositories and a little of Boostrap 5 to create a fast and reliable game environment.

Due to safety reasons (yours, primarily), this site will not store any personal data or your activity on the site. Why would we want to anyway? ¯\_(ツ)_/¯

The only information the site will store on your web browser (cookies files) is session essentials (only game-related information), which will be deleted right after the session.

At first, you need a kendama (a physical one). If you are still reading this, we assume you are unfamiliar with kendama or just started your journey with skill toys. Although there are a lot of kendamas manufacturers, we strongly recommend starting with the most popular companys' products (like Sweets Kendama, Kendama USA, Krom) so you don't need to care about the toy's quality.

If you already have one, you can choose two types of games:

Game of K.E.N is a game manager to control the play flow. It comes with:

  • Contest menu, where you can add players, configure the game settings and see the contest results.
  • The game screen, where you can save and track round progress.

Remember that your progress will be irrevocably deleted after exiting Contest Menu.

Random trick is a trick picker with Kendama Word Cup sets from 2014 to 2019. You can filter by trick level (1-10), year or both! App also provides a simple trick-tracker so they won't duplicate during play.
