TRAINING GAME

Training game settings

You will train with a bot player on Kendama World Cup (2014-2019) tricks list. All training games will repeat the same pattern:

  • In the beginning, the game will randomly choose who will start.
  • The first player chooses a random trick and tries to repeat it.
  • Despite the first player's performance, the second player also needs to repeat the trick.
    • If only one player repeats the trick, he will receive a point.
    • If both players fail or manage to repeat the trick, the round should be repeated (up to 3 rounds) until case "a" occurs.
    • If case "a" doesn't occur in 3 rounds, no one will get the point, and new trick will be selected.
